Photon-Stream (phs)

Single photon event representation for Imaging Atmospheric Cherenkov Telescopes (IACTs)

Public

Crab Nebula

High quality observations of the Crab Nebula in November 2013. Free to use for everyone. Please give us some feedback (biland@phys.ethz.ch) and cite our photon-stream proceeding and our general FACT reference paper.

Simulations (sim)

The FACT responses to simulated air-shower observations. This is the diffuse ceres_12.rc simulation pass.

Private

Observations (obs)

All observed air-shower events of FACT. About 1e9 air-shower records containing about 3e12 single photons. See ./obs/runstatus.csv to see the photon-stream integrity.
